---
title: 'Browse match history with /history'
description: 'View paginated guild-wide match history in Discord. Each page shows up to five match embeds with team rosters, rating deltas, scores, and timestamps.'
---

The `/history` command displays past match results for the whole server, ordered from most recent to oldest. It gives your community a shared record of every game that has been recorded through the bot.

## Paginated embed view

Results are split into pages of up to 5 matches each. Use the navigation buttons that appear below the message to move between pages. Each match is rendered as its own embed within the page containing:

- **Team A** and **Team B** roster fields, showing a Discord mention for every player along with their rating delta in parentheses (e.g. `@alice (+2.30)`, `@bob (-1.15)`). A positive delta means the player's `mu` increased; negative means it decreased.
- **Match title** showing the final score (e.g. `3 - 1`) or, for draws, `0 - 0`.
- **Timestamp** showing the day and time the result was recorded (e.g. `Saturday, Apr 18 @ 02:45 PM`).
- A 👑 crown icon on the winning team's field name.

If no matches have been recorded yet, the bot responds with "No Matches Found."

<Tip>
  To see match history filtered to a single player — including their rating graph and win rate — right-click that player in Discord, select **Apps**, then choose **Rating and History**.
</Tip>
